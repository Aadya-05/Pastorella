Our Menu

Spaghetti Alfredo Al Limone

Spaghetti Alfredo al Limone is a zesty twist on the classic Alfredo, combining the richness of butter and cream with the bright, refreshing flavor of fresh lemon juice and zest. This dish offers a perfect balance of creamy and tangy notes, making it a delightful and uplifting pasta experience.

Penne Alfredo Classico

Penne Alfredo Classico is a traditional Italian pasta dish featuring penne tossed in a rich and creamy Alfredo sauce made from butter, heavy cream, and Parmesan cheese. The sauce clings perfectly to the ridged penne, delivering a comforting and satisfying meal.

Penne Arrabbiata Con Verdure

If you like it spicy, our Penne Arrabbiata is for you. Made with a fiery tomato sauce, garlic, red chili peppers, and other fresh veggies, this dish packs a punch.

Spaghetti Arrabbiata Con Funghi

Farfalle Primavera

Farfalle Primavera is a vibrant pasta dish that features bow-tie farfalle pasta tossed with a medley of fresh, seasonal vegetables like bell peppers, cherry tomatoes, and zucchini. The dish is typically light and flavorful, often dressed with a light garlic and olive oil sauce or a delicate cream, highlighting the freshness of the vegetables.

Macaroni Alla Rosa

Macaroni Alla Rosa is a delightful pasta dish where macaroni is enveloped in a creamy pink sauce made from a blend of tomato and heavy cream. This rich and slightly tangy sauce offers a velvety texture and a harmonious balance of flavors, making it a comforting and indulgent choice.
